About Us

Our Team

Pictured are several members of our team recieving the "2022 Mayor's Sports Award" for their, and the teams, outstanding success with the programme.Our remarkable group of volunteers have ignited a vibrant local sports programme in our community. With passion and dedication, this team of compassionate individuals has come together to create a safe and engaging space for young people with additional needs to connect, learn, and grow. Their collective expertise and diverse backgrounds enable them to provide a wide range of activities that cater to the diverse interests and needs of our group. From sports to mentorship, this group of volunteers tirelessly works to foster a nurturing environment where young minds can thrive. Through their selfless commitment, they have not only established a sports programme but also a sense of belonging and empowerment for the young children of our community.
